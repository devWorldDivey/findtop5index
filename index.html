NSE Daily Snapshot Analyzer

How to use this tool:

  1. Download the "Daily Snapshot" (`ind_close_all...csv`) from the NSE India website.
  2. Click "Choose File" and select the downloaded CSV.
  3. A pie chart showing the percentage contribution of top positive movers will appear, along with sorted data tables.
